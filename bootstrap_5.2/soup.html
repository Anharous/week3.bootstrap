Soup Recipes

~Tomato Soup~

-----Ingredients-----

  1. 1 tablespoon unsalted butter or margarine
  2. 1 tablespoon olive oil
  3. 1 onion, thinly sliced
  4. 2 large garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
  5. 2 (28 ounce) cans whole peeled tomatoes
  6. 1 cup water
  7. 1 tablespoon sugar
  8. ¼ teaspoon celery seed
  9. ¼ teaspoon dried oregano
  10. 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  11. salt and ground black pepper to taste

----------Instructions----------

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat butter and olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-low heat. Cook onion and garlic until onion is soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes, water, sugar, celery seed, oregano, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and purée with an immersion blender. Reheat soup until warm and season with salt and pepper.

Tips

  • Use Italian San Marzano tomatoes for the best flavor.
  • This soup can be made vegan by substituting margarine or 2 tablespoons of olive oil for the butter.

~Spiced Carrot and Onion Soup~

Ingredients

  1. 1 Onion , finely chopped
  2. 2 Carrots , cut into small cubes
  3. 1 inch Ginger , thinly sliced
  4. 1 teaspoon Cumin powder
  5. 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder
  6. 1/4 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder
  7. 2 cups Vegetable stock
  8. 1 cup Milk
  9. 1 tablespoon Butter
  10. Coriander Leaves , small bunch, finely chopped
  11. Salt , to taste
  12. Sugar , to taste
  13. Black pepper powder , freshly crushed to taste
  14. Fresh cream , or coconut cream for garnish

Instructions

  1. To begin making the Spiced Carrot and Onion Soup Recipe; heat the butter in sauce pan, saute the onions and ginger until soft. Add the carrots and saute covered for a minute or two until it gets cooked.
  2. In a small bowl combine the spice powders along with a little water to make a paste and add it to the onion and carrots. Stir well to combine and saute for a minute.
  3. Blend all the ingredients by adding the stock and milk to make a smooth puree.
  4. Once blended, pour the carrot onion soup into the saucepan and give the soup a quick boil. Check for the salt and spice levels and adjust to suit your taste.
  5. To Serve the Carrot and Onion Soup garnish it with some cream and coriander leaves and served with a toasted bread.
  6. Serve Spiced Carrot and Onion Soup Recipe with Summer Vegetable Lettuce & Pasta Salad Recipe With Croutons for a light lunch or dinner

~Vegetable Soup~

Ingredients

  1. ½ tablespoon-----Oil (or butter)
  2. ½ tablespoon-----Garlic (fine chopped or minced)
  3. ½ tablespoon-----Ginger (fine chopped or minced)
  4. 2 sprigs---------Spring Onions (greens, or scallions)
  5. 2 sprigs---------Spring Onion (whites, or scallions)
  6. ¼ cup------------Carrots (fine chopped)
  7. ¼ cup------------Peas
  8. ¼ cup------------Sweet Corn
  9. ¼ cup------------French Beans
  10. ½ cup------------Cabbage (fine chopped)
  11. 2 ½ cups---------Water (or vegetable stock)
  12. 1 tablespoon-----Corn Starch (or 3 tbsp oats powder or 1 tbsp arrowroot powder, optional, refer notes)
  13. ¼ cup------------Water (to mix with flour)
  14. ¼ teaspoon-------Salt (adjust to taste)
  15. 1 teaspoon-------Sugar (optional)
  16. 1 teaspoon-------Lemon Juice (or vinegar, apple cider)
  17. ½ teaspoon-------Red Chilli Flakes
  18. ½ teaspoon-------Pepper Powder (crushed pepper)
  19. 1 ---------------Small Bay Leaf (optional)

Instructions

  1. Chop or mince ginger and garlic.
  2. Fry garlic, ginger, bay leaf (optional) and spring onion whites for a minute.
  3. Then add all the other veggies and saute for 2 mins on a high flame to bring out the aroma.
  4. Pour water or stock. I used water here
  5. When the veggies turn slightly tender, pour the corn strach to the boiling vegetable soup. Stir continuously and cook until it turns slightly thick.
  6. Add salt and sugar
  7. Add then crushed pepper.
  8. Turn off when the soup turns slightly thick. It thicknes further after cooling. Very thick soup tastes starchy so do not thicken it a lot. The soup must be free flowing and not very thick
  9. Prefer to add apple cider vinegar when the soup cools down a bit. Taste the soup and add more salt if desired.
  10. Add red chili flakes and more spring onion greens
  11. Serve Indian-Chinese vegetable soup hot or warm.
